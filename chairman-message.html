Chairman's Message

We believe that every child is gifted, and with a little encouragement and grooming, he can reach his true potential and become a better version of himself.

At Yash Global School, we are dedicated to providing the nurturing environment and guidance that allows each child to flourish, both academically and as a well-rounded individual. Our commitment to their growth extends beyond the classroom, encompassing character development, extracurricular pursuits, and the values that shape responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Mr. Nitin Kumar Gupta
Chairman, Yash Global School

Princpal's Message

Education main first objective is to became a good human being and build a strong character and citizen.

At Yash Global School, we're dedicated to instilling these values, ensuring that our students not only excel academically but also become compassionate and ethical members of society. As the principal, I am proud to lead a team of educators who share this vision, and together, we are committed to shaping a brighter future for our students. We believe that a well-rounded education goes beyond textbooks, encompassing essential life skills, empathy, and social responsibility. It is our mission to empower our students to make a positive impact on the world, guided by knowledge and a strong moral compass.

Mr. Praveen Kumar Gupta
Principal, Yash Global School
