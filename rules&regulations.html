Rules & Regulations

To ensure a harmonious and productive academic environment, Yash Global School has established a set of rules and regulations for our students. These guidelines are designed to create a safe, respectful, and inclusive community where each student can thrive academically and personally. We believe that understanding and abiding by these rules is not just about compliance but also about cultivating responsibility and integrity, qualities that will serve our students well throughout their lives.

  • Punctuality is essential; students arriving late will not be allowed.
  • The school diary is mandatory, used for recording daily homework and teacher instructions. If lost, a replacement can be purchased by paying a certain amount as per the school's administration.
  • Silence and single file walking are required in corridors and on stairs.
  • During recess or games period, students should not stay in classrooms or corridors without special permission.
  • Participation in club activities is expected from all students.
  • Maintain cleanliness by using designated bins; refrain from littering the campus.
  • Avoid bringing valuables or money to school.
  • Students under the care of servants should not leave school before the servants arrive.
  • The prescribed school uniform must be worn when entering the school premises.
  • Display good behavior, greet teachers and elders, and avoid using harsh or foul language.
  • Mobile phones are prohibited. If found so, the student will have to pay a penalty. In case of emergency the school phone may be used.
  • As a rule, children will not be permitted to leave the school to attend any social function during school hours. However in case of an emergency, a written application should be submitted by the parents with the Principal.
  • Care must be taken of the school property. Any damage done or noticed should be reported at once, even if it is caused by accident the damage has to be compensated in cash/kind.
  • Violating school rules may lead to dismissal.

  • All the PTMs must be attended by parents or the guardian whose name has been mentioned in admission form.
  • Fee must be deposited strictly as per schedule.

Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to these principles, as we work hand in hand to nurture the potential in every student and prepare them for a bright tomorrow.
